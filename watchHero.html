What is the experiment?

It is a recreation of Apple's WatchOS hero section which rotates when the user scrolls down.


Challenge(s) I came across

The first challenge was the circular orientation of the "watch faces", which I was able to resolve by finding a very clever piece of code, courtesy of Ana Tudor.


Secondly, positioning the circular div such that it is "fixed" and rotates till a certain scroll length, after which it scrolls up was tricky to figure out. In version one of this experiment, I solved this by keeping the position of the div fixed and after scrolling down that certain length the position became absolute. This was a very hacky and janky method especially since it required that I change the height of the wrapper div as well. Eventually this was resolved by using "position: sticky", which I previously did not have a good grasp and understanding of.


Lastly, the design of the watch faces. Although not a "challenge" per se, it was particularly time consuming.
